Australia PR Points Calculator

The various Australia Immigration programs have been strategically designed for the selection of only the highly qualified applicants. Specific parameters have been conceptualized for ensuring that only chosen profiles that can contribute significantly to the economy of the nation get an invitation to progress in their PR Process for Immigration to Australia.

Australian points calculator is one such mechanism that awards points to candidates depending on various factors for determining their potential. It is a tool that helps aspiring immigrants in the estimation of tier potential to fulfill the requirements for Australia PR. Applicants who can score at least 65 or plus points become eligible to file their application in the Skill Select - the portal of the Government of Australia that determines the eligibility of immigration candidates.

The Skilled Immigration Program of Australia uses a system based on points termed as the Skill Select which is also known as Australia PR points calculator. It is designed to attract highly skilled and qualified candidates who wish to reside and work in Australia. The system has been designed in such a manner that it ensures the selection of only those applicants who possess the precise skills and experience for addressing the labor market shortages.

Various types of categories are available through the Skilled Immigration Visas that specify scoring of minimum points for the candidates to qualify for the Australia Permanent Residency Visa. The Australia Visa categories for which the point's calculator is widely used are:

Skilled Independent Subclass 189 Visa

Skilled Nominated Subclass 190 Visa

Employer Nomination Subclass 186 Visa
